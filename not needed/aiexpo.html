AI EXPO

school project

OVERVIEW

For this project, we were tasked with selecting an event or conference and designing a poster that strictly adhered to typography rules. I chose the AI4 2024 event, North America’s largest artificial intelligence industry gathering.

CONCEPTUALIZATION

The concept behind my poster design was to reflect the cutting-edge and innovative spirit of the AI4 2024 event. I aimed to convey the event's focus on advanced technology and AI through a clean, modern typographic approach. By emphasizing text over imagery, the design draws attention to the importance of the event itself, aligning with the forward-thinking nature of the AI community.

DESIGN PROCESS

I began the design process by focusing solely on typography, deliberately excluding images to ensure that the text stood as the primary visual element. This allowed me to experiment with various styles, sizes, and layouts to establish a strong visual hierarchy. I created multiple design variations, testing different arrangements and typographic treatments to see how each affected the overall composition and message. This iterative process enabled me to refine the poster, balancing the need for clarity with the desire to capture the innovative essence of the AI4 2024 event.
