Vroam


School Group Project

Conceptualization

The idea originated from a desire to create a mobile app that offers virtual travel experiences, especially for those facing physical or financial limitations. While developing the concept, it became clear that enhancing the sense of community would add significant value. This led to the integration of social media features, allowing users not only to explore the world virtually but also to share their experiences and connect with others, enriching the overall journey.

Personas Development

Personas were crafted early in the process to represent our diverse target audience, including individuals with disabilities, budget-conscious travelers, and tech-savvy explorers. These personas played a crucial role in shaping the app’s design, ensuring that the features and user experience aligned closely with the real needs and desires of our users.

Wireframing & Prototyping

Initial wireframes were sketched to map out the app’s basic structure, navigation, and key functionalities. These low-fidelity wireframes served as a foundation, which was then developed into high-fidelity versions that detailed the visual design and interactions. Interactive prototypes were created from these wireframes, providing a tangible way to test and refine the app’s flow and design elements, leading to informed adjustments based on user interaction.

Usability Testing & Iteration

Usability testing was conducted with five individuals who share a passion for travel. Their feedback highlighted areas for improvement and was instrumental in refining the app’s design. Iterative changes were made to the user interface, enhancing intuitiveness and optimizing the integration of social media features. These refinements ensured a more seamless and engaging user experience.

Final

After extensive testing and multiple iterations, the final product emerged as Vroam—an immersive virtual travel app that brings global exploration to users’ fingertips. The inclusion of robust social features fosters a sense of connection and community, making the experience not just about travel, but about sharing and connecting with others who share the same passion.
